Higher Home

  • Guests 8
  • Compartments 5
  • Bathrooms2
  • Rooms3

It is located in Cornino, in the seafront. It is part of an Ancient Rural landscape on the borderlines of Mount Cofano Reservation. The sea is very clean and it’s 100 metres far. The home furnishings are designed in an old-fashioned Sicilian style with references to the Sicilian mythology. Arches, wood oven, kitchens in lava and Giarre (ancient vases) will remind you of the Temples of the Elimi. Indeed, you can enjoy techonology too thanks to TV lcd, wi-fi, air conditioned and washing machine. The kitchen offers all the necessary utensils. Linen is included. Guests can benefit of the barbecue and the wood oven.
